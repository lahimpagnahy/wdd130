Madagascar

A little village in Madagascar


Madagascar, the world's fourth-largest island, lies off Africa's southeastern coast. It boasts unique biodiversity, with over 90% of its wildlife found nowhere else. The island features rainforests, deserts, and beaches. Despite its rich natural resources, Madagascar faces challenges like political instability and poverty. Its economy relies on agriculture, mining, and tourism, with ecotourism growing rapidly.
